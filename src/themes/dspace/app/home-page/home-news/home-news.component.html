BYSMoH E-Library

The BYSMoH E-Library is an advanced platform, enabling seamless access to a wide array of resources. It allows organizations and individuals to:

  • Easily upload and manage documents, audio, video, datasets, and their corresponding metadata for streamlined accessibility.
  • Provide global visibility to content through robust indexing with Google Scholar and OAI-PMH integration.
  • Generate reliable and permanent URLs for resources, with options for handle.net and DataCite DOI integrations.
